SMP in action

Upon the platform, SMP is building CINQ - a series of first-of-breed intelligent perfume diffusers.

CINQ creates custom fragrances based on the deep analysis of consumer's preferences and usage patterns.

CINQ combines AI with a set of 5 fragrance multichannel diffusers to jointly create up to 8000 unique scent combinations.

Learning from consumer interactions with the diffuser through a connected App, CINQ autonomously forecasts the scent combination that matches consumer's personalized fragrance profile.

Forget about conventional diffusers that cause smell fatigue, CINQ never gets old by assembling personalized fragrances for every consumer.

Deep personalization is achieved by AI-powered selection of 5 cartridges out of 24 basic ones. This allows to have 5 million unique combinations of cartridges, ensuring a consumer will find theirs.

Read more about how CINQ revolutionizes scent creation and pre-order at www.getcinq.com
